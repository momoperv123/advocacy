Which of the following are common signs of depression?

Persistent sadness or low mood
✅ This is a common symptom of depression, characterized by ongoing feelings of sadness or a consistently low mood.

Increased energy and enthusiasm
❌ Increased energy and enthusiasm are not typical signs of depression. Depression often leads to decreased energy and enthusiasm.

Loss of interest in previously enjoyed activities
✅ A loss of interest in activities that were once enjoyed is a common symptom of depression. It's called anhedonia.

Difficulty concentrating or making decisions
✅ Cognitive difficulties, including trouble concentrating and making decisions, are often experienced by individuals with depression.

What are potential symptoms of anxiety?

Rapid heartbeat and shortness of breath
✅ These physical symptoms are common in anxiety and are often associated with the "fight or flight" response.

Decreased levels of stress and worry
❌ Anxiety is characterized by increased levels of stress and worry, not decreased levels.

Muscle tension and restlessness
✅ Muscle tension and restlessness are common physical symptoms of anxiety.

Deep, restful sleep every night
❌ Anxiety can lead to sleep disturbances, including difficulty falling asleep or staying asleep.

Which of these factors can contribute to stress?

Regular exercise and a balanced diet
❌ Regular exercise and a balanced diet are generally considered stress-reducing factors.

Financial difficulties
✅ Financial difficulties can be a significant source of stress for individuals and families.

Supportive social connections
❌ Supportive social connections typically help reduce stress rather than contribute to it.

Work-life balance
❌ Achieving a healthy work-life balance is often seen as a way to reduce stress, not contribute to it.

Indicate the following statements about seeking help for mental health concerns.

It's a sign of weakness to seek help.
❌ Seeking help for mental health concerns is not a sign of weakness. It takes courage to acknowledge and address mental health issues.

Seeking help is a courageous step toward healing.
✅ Seeking help for mental health concerns is a brave and proactive step toward improving one's well-being.

Only "serious" mental health issues require professional help.
❌ Mental health concerns, regardless of severity, can benefit from professional help. Early intervention can prevent issues from worsening.

Friends and family can be valuable sources of support.
✅ Friends and family can provide essential emotional support for individuals dealing with mental health challenges. Their support can be invaluable.
